About us

We started AlertEye because seconds matter.

A camera that only records is useful after the fact. We wanted one that speaks up while there is still time to act.

The problem we kept seeing

Most places already have cameras. What they don't have is someone watching every feed, every minute. Fires take hold, incidents escalate, and the footage only gets reviewed once the damage is done.

We believed the cameras already in place could do far more. With modern detection models, a feed can be understood as it happens, not just stored for later.

What AlertEye does

AlertEye runs alongside your existing cameras and checks every frame for fire and smoke, weapons, and road accidents. When it is confident a threat is real, it calls your phone, sends a push notification, and emails you with the live location. The whole thing takes seconds.

How we think about it

Detection should be quiet until it isn't. We tune for confirmed threats rather than constant noise, so an alert from AlertEye is something you can trust and act on immediately.
